Tiger Scheduling

A Simple Solution to your Schedluing Needs

Tiger Schduling is a basic scheduling app developed as a means to help me better keep track of my day to day tasks. Having used Apple's reminder app, for the longest of time, I wanted to create an app that simplified the whole process and as well as learn how to develop iOS Applications.

The Answer for your Day-to-Day Tasks!

While simple, this app will have a huge impact on your day-to-day life. Everyone forgets something, wether it be an item on a grocery list, or a forgetting the time of when you need to leave, or even forgetting to remind yourself to do the simplest of things. You can use this app to remind yourself of pretty much anything without the hassle of having to navigate a complex UI. to do so.

Great on the Go!

Need a quick reminder to make on-the-fly? Look no where else! Tap in to this nifty little app to keep yourself on track! You can make reminders for same day events with it's nifty time dial where you can plan your day accordingly. With Tiger Scheduling, you will conquer the day!
